LocAR.js tester - privacy notice

Before using this tester, please agree to the following privacy notice. The app will send your GPS data to this server for logging/debugging purposes. It will send and log the following:

  • The date and time at which GPS listening begins;
  • Your initial location;
  • The location of each new GPS position;
  • Your GPS accuracy.
It will also give you a unique numerical session ID and associate each reading with your session ID. However it does not collect any personal information (name, gmail account, etc).
